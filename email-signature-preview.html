How to use for email signatures:

  1. Best method: Use an online SVG to PNG converter (like convertio.co or cloudconvert.com) to convert the SVG file to PNG at 2x resolution
  2. Alternative: Take a screenshot of the logo above at 2x zoom level
  3. Upload: Host the PNG on your website (e.g., middlez.com/images/email-signature.png)
  4. Email signature HTML: 
    <img src="https://middlez.com/images/email-signature.png" alt="Middle Z" width="120" height="30" style="display: block; max-width: 120px;">

Why PNG? Most email clients don't support SVG, so PNG ensures maximum compatibility across Outlook, Gmail, Apple Mail, etc.
